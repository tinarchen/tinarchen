Objective

I wanted to improve the user experience in ESPN’s App for Fantasy App by identifying problems and opportunities.

My Role

I researched and explored ways to improve the user experience by identifying problems and providing solutions to the overarching issues.

Duration

One Week

INTRODUCTION

How might I create a more informative app but provide a balanced space?

ESPN’s Fantasy App is the #1 app for fantasy football lovers alongside other sports. It updates you on the latest news of players, you can draft and pick players throughout the season.

Even though it is the #1 app for Fantasy lovers (including myself), I wanted find out how I can improve Fantasy’s usability and enhance the user’s journey.

Information Heirarchy

Pushing the most important numbers, data, and call to action buttons up to the front.

Interactive Cards

Allowing player’s to view big data quickly while also providing them analytical stats that are secondary.

Streamlined News

Removing text heavy descriptions and replacing it with quick and informative videos and big numbers, instead of long paragraphs.

Player Match Up

Simplifying adding/ dropping players with a “Player Match Up Screen” that allows for comparison between players.

Final Design and Interactive Prototype

Player’s Information Before

There are text heavy descriptions and boxed information that makes the space feel small. The user also has to click on the player’s name to access this pop up screen which is extremely long.

Player’s Information After

In my modification, you can easily access the player’s info and everything is condensed to the information that is most important when making a decision about benching players. In text heavy areas, I replaced them with recent news videos or big number stats that will pop up for the user.

Adding/ Dropping Players Before

With the current screens, it is hard for the user to make comparisons simultaneously between two players.

Adding/ Dropping Players After

To simplify the process of adding/ dropping players to the roster, I added the “Player Match Up Screen”. When the name of the player is picked, instead on their player info screen- you get a comparison between the player you are trying to add and drop. This allows you to see clearly their average points and outlook in Fantasy.

I still wanted to keep the UI the same, but introduce colors for comparison of the two players. Users can see what the player’s average points, stats, and outlook in order to make their better decisions for adding/ dropping players.
