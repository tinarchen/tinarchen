About Me
In the pursuit of becoming a product designer I am at my final year at Carnegie Mellon University studying Human Computer Interaction and Business Administration. By studying the intersection between these two disciplines, I can shape my designs beyond pure aesthetics- digging deep into the “why” and “how” of business or real-world challenges. My studies focus particularly on understanding human emotions and creating experiences that make an impact on the world through systematic design.

When I’m not lost in my work, you’ll find me playing with my dog or messing around in some doodles. Or you might find me eating!

Currently, I am currently open to opportunities to learn, grow, and impact.

Thank you for visiting my website. If you have any questions, feel free to reach me at trchen@andrew.cmu.edu.
